About Us

Incorporated in 2000, BSWL commenced production in 2008–09 and today operates five units across Maharashtra.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become India’s most efficient sugar manufacturer and contribute to the well‑being of society.

Mission

  • Excel in production of sugar and allied products.
  • Satisfy expectations of customers, employees, and suppliers.
  • Adopt new technologies and processes for continuous improvement.

Director Desk

Dr. Tanaji Jayawant Sawant, an Electrical Engineer, started his career in education and later founded several institutions under Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal. With strong roots in farming, he established Bhairavnath Sugar Works Limited and expanded operations across multiple units.

Introduction

BSWL is an integrated group engaged in sugar manufacturing and allied products with co‑generation at Sonari, Vihal, and Lavangi. The total licensed/installed capacity is 12,250 TCD with ~53.5 MW power generation.
