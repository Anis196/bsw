Products & Processes

Sugar

Operations across five units (Sonari, Vihal, Lavangi, Washi, Alegaon) with total crushing capacity of 12,250 TCD. Grades: M30, S30, Super S.

Sugar manufacturing process
  1. Harvest, weigh, and prepare cane (knives & shredders).
  2. Extract juice via milling; send bagasse to boilers.
  3. Clarify juice (lime & SO₂), settle, filter, and evaporate to syrup.
  4. Crystallize under vacuum; centrifuge to separate molasses.
  5. Dry, bag, and store sugar.

Co‑generation (Bagasse‑based Power)

Four power plants (Sonari, Vihal, Lavangi, Alegaon) with aggregate capacity ~53 MW. Surplus exported under EPA with MSEDCL.

  • Sonari: 14.5 MW (2008–09)
  • Vihal: 12 MW (2011–12)
  • Lavangi: 14.5 MW (2013–14)
  • Alegaon: 12.5 MW

Distillery / Ethanol

30 KLPD plant at Sonari producing industrial alcohol/ethanol using molasses with separate boiler & turbine for steam and power.

Allied Products

Fly Ash

Residual ash from bagasse combustion; reused in agriculture and as substitute fuel.

Molasses

By‑product used for alcohol, yeast, cattle feed, and value‑added chemicals.

Pressmud

Filter cake used as organic manure; can be composted with distillery spent wash.

Bagasse

Fibrous residue used for steam & power; also a raw material for paper industry.

BSWL

Integrated sugar & allied products manufacturer with co-generation and distillery capabilities.
