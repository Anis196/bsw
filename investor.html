Code of Conduct & Ethics

  • Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose and seek prior approval.
  • Protect company assets and reputation; act with integrity.
  • Ensure health, safety, and environmental compliance.
  • Maintain confidentiality and comply with applicable laws.
  • No misuse of insider information; disciplinary actions for violations.

CSR Policy

Focus on Education, Environment, Health, and poverty eradication. Company spends at least 2% of average net profits on CSR with programs near manufacturing units.

Innovation

Continuous R&D in agriculture, biotechnology, and manufacturing—adopting latest instruments and practices to improve yield and quality.

Sugar Cane Harvesting

Mechanized harvesters (since 2014) and a transport network (bullock carts, trucks, tractor trolleys) to optimize seasonality and recovery.
